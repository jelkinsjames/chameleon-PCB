MCHStreamer I2S+Toslink Firmware

-Format: I2S Toslink
-Inputs: 8 I2S channels + stereo TOSLink In
-Outputs: 8 I2S channels + stereo SPDIF & TOSLink Out
-I2S Bits per Channel: Input@24/32, Output@24/32
-TOSLINK Bits per channel: 24
-Sampling Frequency: 44100, 48000, 88200, 96000, 176400, 192000

Notes: MCHStreamer Lite Toslink input will not work
